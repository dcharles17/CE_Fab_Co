Expert Metal Fabrication in Cache Valley Since Day One

CE Fab Co is Logan, Utah's premier destination for professional laser engraving and MIG welding services. Located in the heart of Cache Valley, we've built our reputation on delivering exceptional craftsmanship, precision metalwork, and personalized service to both residential and commercial clients throughout Cache County.

Why Choose CE Fab Co for Your Metal Fabrication Needs?

What sets us apart in the Cache Valley metal fabrication industry is our commitment to quality and attention to detail. Whether you need custom railings for your Logan home, structural steel work for a commercial project, or intricate laser engraving for branding purposes, we bring the same level of expertise and care to every job.

Our state-of-the-art equipment includes professional-grade MIG welding machines capable of handling steel, aluminum, and stainless steel projects of any scale. Our precision laser engraving technology allows us to create intricate designs, logos, serial numbers, and artistic details on metal surfaces with unmatched accuracy.
