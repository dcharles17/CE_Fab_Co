How much does laser engraving cost in Logan Utah?

Laser engraving costs vary based on the size, complexity, and material of your project. Simple text engraving on small items can start as low as $20-30, while larger or more intricate designs range from $50-200+. We offer free quotes for all laser engraving projects in Cache Valley. Contact us with your design specifications for accurate pricing.

What metals can you MIG weld?

Our MIG welding services in Logan cover a wide range of metals including mild steel, stainless steel, and aluminum. We can handle various thicknesses and project types from thin sheet metal to heavy structural steel. Our welders are experienced with different material combinations and can recommend the best approach for your specific Cache County project.

Do you serve areas outside of Logan Utah?

Yes! While we're based in Logan, we proudly serve all of Cache Valley and Cache County including Smithfield, Hyde Park, North Logan, Wellsville, Hyrum, Mendon, Providence, and surrounding communities. We'll provide a clear timeline estimate when we quote your project and keep you updated throughout the process.
